LABORATORY QUESTIONS


Part A – Technical Questions

1. What are the five columns in the dataset?
     Date, Region, Category, Product, Sales
   
2. What data type is assigned to the “Sales” column?
     The Sales column should be assigned the Decimal Number data type.
   
3. Which Power BI view allows you to see raw data?
     The Data View allows you to see raw data.
   
4. What chart type is best for showing trends over time?
     A Line Chart is best for showing trends over time.

   
5. What aggregation is automatically applied to Sales?
     The aggregation automatically applied to Sales is Sum (Sum of Sales).


Part B – Analytical Questions

6. Which region has the highest total sales?
     The West Region has the highest total sales.
7. Which category has the lowest performance?
     Based on the category chart, Clothing (or the smallest bar shown in your chart) has the lowest performance.
8. Are sales increasing, decreasing, or stable?
      Sales are decreasing. There is a clear downward trend over time.
9. If you were a manager, which region would you prioritize?
      As a manager, I would prioritize the West Region because it generates the highest revenue and shows strong performance.
10. Provide one actionable recommendation based on the data.
      One actionable recommendation is to:
      Focus on strategies to stop the sales decline, such as launching promotions, improving marketing campaigns, or boosting sales in lower-performing regions and categories.
